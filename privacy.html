Privacy

Our site doesn't use trackers! It is private! Our contact pages are HubSpot forms. Any information you enter on these pages will never be distributed.

Ads hosted with us do not contain trackers. We use Kutt for ad links. This is a link shortening service that we have chosen to use because it records very little data. It records when that specific link is clicked (ad clicks), country of origin, web browser, and what operating system said browser is developed for. All collected data is kept secure and private. Ad clicks are shared with their respective advertisers. We do not collect any personally identifiable information.

We do not publisize who purchases ads from us. However, all ads can be viewed from the dashboard. This can increase ad views. We do this so that customers and potential customers can see that our ads deliver results.

We keep our customers' information secure and private. We never share personally identifiable information.

You can request to have your ad hidden from the dashboard.

Email us to have your account and its data deleted.
